Overview

Purpose

My purpose for this site is to create an auction website for cattle auctions. I want it to be display cattle numbers, the farmers information, cattle information, and the price.

Audience

My intended audience is mostly for older people. Auctions can be loud and very confusing so this webpage will help them be able to get all the information they need so they can have a better time at the auction.

Content

Home page

Auctions can be very confusing and difficult for a lot of people. There is a lot going on, the auctioneer is talking away making no sense, hands are flying up all the time, cattle is moving quickly, and nothing is making sense. That is where my page comes in. My page is going to help everyone out and make life easier. People will come here to help them with there auction expereince. this page will have info about how to bid properly, cattle information, and anything else that will be important. The first thing that will pop up on my homepage is where I will explain my purpose and who the website is for. the purpose of my website is simple yet important. My purpose is to provide people who have a hard time at auctions the support and resources they need to have a good time at a cattle auction. This page will have tons of infomation that is needed in order to be succesful at an auction. All information regarding the farmer will be easily found, all information about the cattle that is important will be shown and can be easily found, and lastly all information regarding the auction times and rules will be provided and it will be easily found. After that my audience statement will be below my purpose. Here I will explain that my website is meant for those new to auctions. Here they can view the rules about auctions allowing them to know how to properly bid and receive the cattle the buy. This website is also for those hard of hearing, and older people. Here they can read the information about the cattle and farmers. Now they know which cattle they want to buy and all the have to do is find the number and cattle and bid.

Cattle information

Here is all of the infomation about any and all farmers that are selling cattle. Here we have the farmers information. We provide the phone number state where they came from, and anything else that is relative to selling any cattle. We will also have the herd information. This Page will show the amount of cows being sold. It will also provide the average herd weight, what they have been eating, and any other important information. We will also note any genetic defects found in the cattle, any recent sickness, or anything else that may be of concern to a farmer that wants to buy the cattle and introduce it to the herd. Amongst this information we will provide the auction information. This will consist of there place in line when they will be sold, there auction tags, and any other information that will help identify them so they can easily be spotted.

About Us

Hello my name is Matthew Jensen. I am from a small town called Arco Idaho. There I helped my grandparents farm and ranch. I loved it and it became a major part in my life. After a while I realized there was a problem. Everyone talked about going to cattle auctions, but I never went to one. So finally I managed to convince my parents to let me go so I can experience the amazing cattle acutions that everyone loved. It was an amazing experience until the actual auction part happened. I had a super hard time following along with all the information the auctioneer was saying. So then an idea popped in my head. I can create a website that helps the people who have a hard time at auctions. Thus Jensen auctions was born. I began researching about the best ways to create a website. I also got real peoples advice about how to format the website so it was easier to use. For my education I am attending BYU-I. Here I am studying for a computer degree. I am determined to learn multiple coding languages. So far I have done HTML and python but I plan on learning more in the future.
