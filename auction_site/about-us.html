Hello my name is Matthew Jensen. I am from a small town called Arco Idaho. There I helped my grandparents farm and ranch. I loved it and it became a major part in my life. After a while I realized there was a problem. Everyone talked about going to cattle auctions, but I never went to one. So finally I managed to convince my parents to let me go so I can experience the amazing cattle acutions that everyone loved. It was an amazing experience until the actual auction part happened. I had a super hard time following along with all the information the auctioneer was saying. So then an idea popped in my head. I can create a website that helps the people who have a hard time at auctions. Thus Jensen auctions was born. I began researching about the best ways to create a website. I also got real peoples advice about how to format the website so it was easier to use. For my education I am attending BYU-I. Here I am studying for a computer degree. I am determined to learn multiple coding languages. So far I have done HTML and python but I plan on learning more in the future.

i currently am a idaho resident. I love it so much here. I love the mountains, the land, the people, and the cities.

I first got interested in coding my freshman year of highscool. There I took beginner coding courses and instanlly fell in love. In fact I love it so much I plan on making a career out of it.

Python is my main language. Python's uses are versatile and I plan on using python on most of the stuff I do.
